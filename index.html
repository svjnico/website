FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions Gabby’s Tree Service

Answers to the questions Deer Park and Bay Area homeowners ask most.

How much does tree removal cost in Deer Park? +
Every tree is different, so we cannot give an accurate price without seeing it first. The cost depends on the size of the tree, where it is on your property, what is around it like fences or power lines, and how difficult the access is. That is why we do every estimate in person. We will come out, look at the tree, and give you an honest price on the spot with no hidden fees. There is no charge for the estimate, so call us at (832) 731-6338 and we will set up a time to come take a look.
Do I need a permit to remove a tree in Deer Park? +
It depends on the tree and where it is on your property. Some cities in the Houston area have rules about removing certain trees, especially larger ones near public right of ways. We have been doing tree work in Deer Park and the surrounding Bay Area for over 22 years, so we are familiar with local requirements. When we come out for your free in-person estimate, we can walk you through what applies to your situation and help you figure out the next steps.
Are you licensed and insured? +
Yes, Gabby’s Tree Service is fully licensed and insured. This is one of the most important questions you can ask any tree company before hiring them. If you hire someone who is not insured and a worker gets hurt on your property, or your home or fence gets damaged during the job, you could be held legally responsible for those costs. We carry full liability coverage on every job and are happy to provide proof of insurance before any work begins.
Do you do free estimates over the phone? +
We do all of our estimates in person because tree work cannot be accurately priced from a phone call or a photo. Every tree is different, and things like root systems, proximity to structures, power lines, and the condition of the wood all affect the price and the approach. We will come to your property, look at everything in person, and give you an accurate price right there. The estimate is always free.
How long does it take to remove a tree? +
Most tree removals in the Deer Park area take anywhere from a few hours to a full day depending on the size and location of the tree. A smaller tree in an open yard goes pretty quick. A large oak growing close to a house or fence takes more time because we work carefully to protect everything around it. Our crew handles the full cleanup before we leave, so your yard is spotless by the time we are done.
Do you clean up after the job? +
Every time. Full cleanup is included with every job we do. We bring a mobile wood chipper to the site, chip all the branches, haul off the debris, and make sure your yard looks clean before we leave. We do not leave piles of brush or sawdust for you to deal with. A lot of homeowners in Deer Park and Pasadena have told us they hired another company before that left a mess behind. That will never happen with us.
Can you remove a tree that is close to my house or fence? +
Yes, and this is actually one of the things we are known for. We handle hazardous tree removal near homes, fences, garages, power lines, and other structures regularly across Deer Park, La Porte, and the surrounding area. Our crew uses professional safety gear and equipment and takes the tree down in sections to control exactly where everything falls. We back every job with our Zero Property Damage Guarantee.
What if a tree falls during a storm? Can you come out right away? +
Yes. We provide emergency tree removal for homeowners across Deer Park and the East Houston Bay Area. Storm season here runs from late spring through fall, and we know that a tree on your roof or blocking your driveway cannot wait until next week. Call us at (832) 731-6338 and we will get to you as fast as we can. We handle the full removal and cleanup so you can get back to normal.
Do you use subcontractors? +
Never. Every job is handled by our own experienced in-house crew. You will never have a random crew show up that we do not know or did not train. This is a big deal because when you hire a company that sends subcontractors, you have no idea who is actually working on your property or whether they are properly trained and insured. With us, you always know exactly who is doing the work.
Do I have to pay before the work is done? +
No. We collect payment after the job is complete and you are satisfied with the work. You will know the full price before we start, and there will be no surprise charges when we are finished. We give you upfront pricing with no hidden fees so you know exactly what you are paying for from the beginning.
